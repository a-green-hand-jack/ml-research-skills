## Claim Lifecycle Notes

- `idea`: possible claim, not yet committed.
- `planned`: intended claim, evidence not fully specified.
- `evidence-needed`: evidence requirement is clear but support is insufficient.
- `provisional`: draft uses temporary or incomplete evidence.
- `supported`: verified enough for current paper scope.
- `weakened` / `revised`: scope changed because of results, writing, or review.
- `parked` / `cut`: not part of the active story.
- `final`: locked for submitted or camera-ready paper.
