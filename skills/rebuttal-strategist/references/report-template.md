## Overall Status

- `READY_TO_DRAFT`: issue board and evidence plan ready
- `NEEDS_EXPERIMENTS`: experiments or analyses required before drafting
- `DRAFT_READY`: response draft prepared and internally consistent
- `NEEDS_AUTHOR_DECISION`: strategy depends on author choice or missing evidence
- `INCOMPLETE`: reviews or venue rules unavailable
